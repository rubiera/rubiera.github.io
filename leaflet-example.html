Leaflet Example: Tesla Stores in the Metro DC Area

There is only one Tesla store in Maryland, in the Bethesda-Montgomery Mall. There is also a store in Baltimore-Owings Mills (not pictured here). There is one Tesla store in the District of Columbia-at CityCenterDC. Finally, there are two additional stores in Virginia (there is an additional store in Richmond, not pictured). The Virginia Metro DC stores are located in Vienna and McLean.

In all, there are four Tesla Stores in the Metro DC Area. The popup at each location has the location address and the store and roadside assistance phone numbers.

my_map <- leaflet() %>%
  addTiles()  %>% 
  addMarkers(lat=c(39.024225, 38.916922, 38.931939, 38.917950), 
             lng=c(-77.145667,-77.026191,-77.239896,-77.220654),
             popup=c("Tesla Store <br>
